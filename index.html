Cloud, DevSecOps, and Cybersecurity Consulting

We help organizations of all sizes, across various industries shift-left!

Whether you are looking to modernize your tech stack, migrate to the cloud, automate infrastructure, or understand and improve security, we are here for you.

  • PLANNING AND ESTIMATION: We collaborate with your leadership, development, and engineering teams to identify and break down your processes into stages with measurable objectives for each one. We take into consideration your development standards, functional requirements, and budgetary concerns.
  • COLLABORATIVE DEVELOPMENT AND TESTING: We work with your team (developers, testers, security, etc.) to understand existing development practices and T-shape each team member to enable continuous improvement. We ensure predictable, repeatable processes are automated and transparent to the team.
  • DEPLOYMENT AND MONITORING: We automate your software development practices all the way through build and release to bake in security every step of the way, including continuous monitoring of both your development ecosystem and application stack.


How Do I Know If DevSecOps Is Right For Our Organization?

  • Your security team spends a lot of time patching and remediating.
  • You want better visibility into the security state of your ecosystem.
  • Slow, resource-intensive software releases impede your business agility.
  • Your best engineers are stretched thin maintaining current systems.
  • Your team has a considerable challenge controlling system downtime.

DevSecOps

Cybersecurity

Whether insiders or outsiders, threats are real, do you know your threats? Our team leads you through threat modeling and assessments, helping you understand your risks. We help get your application and platform to ATO faster through automation, auto-remediation, while following cloud-native security best practices and NIST guidance for securing systems.
